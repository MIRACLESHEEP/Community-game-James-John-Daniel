Fight 2 Death!

What is this game?

Fight 2 Death is a 2 player game, where players control avatars and fight each other. You can also choose your difficulty and your background.

How to play

  • Press enter to start
  • Select your preferences for the round
  • Press confirm
  • Play with _____
  • Fight three rounds
  • Have fun!
